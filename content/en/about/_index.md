ALPS is a software package for simulating correlated systems, with codes for classical spin systems, quantum spin systems, bosonic systems, and fermion systems.

## Licensing

All codes under ALPS framework are released under the MIT open-source license.

## Acknowledgements

The ALPS software package has been developed over more than 20 years by researchers from around the world. After long being funded by ETH Zurich and the Simons Foundation, current sponsorship comes from the NSF POSE program.
